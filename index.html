What we stand for

A studio obsessed with calm, capable software.

We believe the most useful apps are the ones that fade into the background. They do their job, they get out of your way, and they never sell you. That philosophy shapes every line of code we ship under the Jingrui Yuetao banner.

Privacy by Default

Every Jingrui Yuetao app keeps your data on the device. No silent syncing, no background tracking, no profiling. You are the customer, not the product.

Engineered for the World

From multilingual UI to right-to-left layouts, our products are designed to feel native in any market. Localized tools, international standards.

Form and Function

Typography, motion, and color are treated as first-class engineering problems. Beautiful products earn their place on your home screen.

Built for Real Use

No flashy demos, no empty feature lists. Every tool is built around a real workflow we have lived ourselves, then stress-tested in the wild.

Modern Stack

Native Android, Jetpack, modern web standards. Our team ships lean, dependency-light, fast applications that age well.

Offline by Design

Whether you are on a flight, a metro, or a mountain, our apps keep working. Connectivity is a luxury, not a requirement.

Our portfolio

Mobile apps on Google Play, designed for daily rituals.

Our matrix spans outdoor offline route parsing, collection asset filing systems, writing inspiration engines, sports load calculators, household bill scanners and recurring habit trackers. Each app is its own universe, calibrated for the moment you need it.
