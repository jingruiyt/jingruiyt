Common questions

Quick answers

What is your typical response time?

We respond to most enquiries within one business day (UTC+8). Critical app-store or production issues are escalated immediately if the subject line begins with URGENT.

Do you sign NDAs before discussing a project?

Yes. We routinely sign mutual NDAs before any substantive technical conversation. We can use your template or ours, whichever is faster.

Where is your data hosted?

All user data for our published mobile applications stays on the device. Any server-side infrastructure we operate is hosted on major cloud providers in the EU, US and Asia-Pacific regions, and is documented in our Privacy Policy.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. Our apps are designed to be usable without an account. For any data that we do process (such as support correspondence or analytics), you can request access, correction or deletion at any time by emailing support@jingruiyt.com.

Do you accept cryptocurrency payments for enterprise contracts?

We can invoice in fiat currency (USD, EUR, GBP, CNY) and accept wire transfer, ACH, SEPA, or major credit cards. For larger contracts we can also explore stablecoin settlement on a case-by-case basis.
